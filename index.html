Illuminating Houses,
Brightening
Your Day.

Bringing light to the darkest corners, EcoLuminate offers a sustainable solution for affordable illumination.

Our kit enables you to build your own solar-powered LED light source, which automatically turns on at night and off during the day. This versatile 2-in-1 kit can be configured into either Roof Mode or Portable Mode, allowing you to switch between the two with ease. By using primarily plastic and paper, we convert disposable items into a functional lighting solution. Priced at just RM25, the kit includes quality components from MR. DIY, combined with cost-free recyclable materials.


Status Quo

○ Rural areas lack reliable, affordable, and sustainable lighting, creating significant financial burdens.

○ Traditional lighting solutions are too expensive or impractical, affecting daily life, education, and safety.

Our Objective

  • Provide sustainable, eco-friendly, and cost-effective lighting solutions.
  • Reduce financial burdens and improve quality of life in rural communities.
  • To encourage DIY (Do-It_Yourself) in order to promote education in STEM as well as innovation.
  • To establish community and school events in creating our kit and donating EcoLuminates all while creating a brighter future for people living in rural areas.
  • To promote recyling in communities by using whatever material at their disposal to create an EcoLuminate from our kit.
  • Adhering to the SDG's (7, 9, 11) to move towards a more stable future for generations to come.
